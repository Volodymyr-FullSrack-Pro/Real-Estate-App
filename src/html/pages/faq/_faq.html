Find answers to the most common questions about our real estate services and properties

How do I start searching for a property?

You can begin your property search by using our search filters at the top of the homepage. Filter by location, property type, price range, and more to find listings that match your criteria. Our advanced search tools make it easy to narrow down options based on your specific needs and preferences.

What documents do I need when buying a property?

Required documents typically include proof of identity, proof of address, proof of income, bank statements, and sometimes tax returns. Additional documents may be needed depending on the specific property and location. Our expert agents will guide you through the entire documentation process.

How long does the buying process take?

The typical property buying process takes 2-3 months from initial offer to completion, but this can vary based on factors such as market conditions, financing arrangements, and legal requirements in different locations. Our team works efficiently to ensure the process moves as smoothly as possible.

What are closing costs and who pays them?

Closing costs are fees associated with completing a real estate transaction, including legal fees, taxes, insurance, and administrative costs. These costs are typically split between buyer and seller according to local customs and negotiated terms. We provide a detailed breakdown of all costs before closing so there are no surprises.

Can I sell my current home and buy a new one simultaneously?

Yes, it's possible to coordinate the sale of your current home with the purchase of a new one. Our agents can help you manage this process, which might involve temporary housing arrangements or contingency clauses in your contracts. We have extensive experience in coordinating these complex transactions.
